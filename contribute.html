Get Involved in Neurodiagnoses

How to Contribute

Neurodiagnoses is an open collaboration for AI-powered CNS diagnostics. We welcome **researchers, AI developers, neuroscientists, and clinicians**.

💡 Why Join?

By participating in Neurodiagnoses, you contribute to **next-generation AI models for diagnosing CNS diseases**.

📌 Ways to Get Involved

  • 💻 **AI Development:** Train and optimize machine learning models.
  • 🧠 **Neuroscience Research:** Contribute to the integration of biomarkers and neuroimaging.
  • 📊 **Data Science:** Work with EEG/MRI/biomarker datasets.
  • 📜 **Scientific Writing:** Help document findings and publish research.
