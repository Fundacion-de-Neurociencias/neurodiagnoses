Contributing to Neurodiagnoses

Neurodiagnoses is an open-source AI diagnostic framework promoted and supported by Fundación de Neurociencias Neurodiagnoses follows a **dual-license model (MIT + Commercial)**.

This project brings together clinicians, researchers, and AI developers to build a next-generation diagnostic system for complex CNS diseases.

🔹 Who Can Contribute?

  • 🩺 Neurologists & Radiologists – Clinical validation & case studies
  • 🤖 AI Researchers & Data Scientists – Machine learning, deep learning, feature engineering
  • 💾 Medical Informatics Specialists – Integrating AI models with clinical workflows
  • 🧬 Neuroscience & Bioinformatics Experts – Biomarker discovery & neuroimaging

💡 Why Join?

By participating in Neurodiagnoses, you contribute to next-generation AI models for diagnosing CNS diseases.

📌 Ways to Get Involved

  • 💻 AI Development: Train and optimize machine learning models.
  • 🧠 Neuroscience Research: Contribute to the integration of biomarkers and neuroimaging.
  • 📊 Data Science: Work with EEG/MRI/biomarker datasets.
  • 📜 Scientific Writing: Help document findings and publish research.
