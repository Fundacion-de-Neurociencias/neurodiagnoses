Get Involved with Neurodiagnoses

Join our collaborative effort to advance AI-powered diagnostics for complex CNS diseases through our interactive contribution guide below.

Introduction

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered diagnostic framework that integrates probabilistic models, neuroimaging, biomarkers, and clinical data to improve diagnostic precision for CNS conditions. This guide provides an overview of our project and helps you get started.

How to Contribute

Get involved by engaging in discussions, contributing code, sharing data and resources, or running your own AI training. Our community thrives on collaboration and innovative contributions.

  • Participate in Discussions: Join our EBRAINS Forum and GitHub Discussions.
  • Contribute to Code Development: Visit our GitHub repository to report issues, suggest features, or submit pull requests.
  • Share Data and Resources: Enhance our diagnostic models by providing datasets, tools, or other valuable resources.
  • Run Your Own AI Training:
    1. Clone the repository from GitHub.
    2. Install the required dependencies as documented on our Wiki.
    3. Access our Jupyter Notebooks for model training and analysis.
  • Stay Updated: Follow us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates.

Workflow & Best Practices

Our workflow is built on a set of well-documented modules and protocols. Explore our resources below:

  • Data Preprocessing: Leverage our automated scripts and protocols to preprocess neuroimaging and biomarker data. Check out our Data Preprocessing module and related documentation under our Docs.
  • Model Integration: For model development and integration, review our integration modules:
    • NDKP Integration
    • MIP Integration
    Also, consult our Documentation for best practices.
  • Biomarker Ontology: Understand our standardized approach to biomarkers by reviewing our Biomarker Ontology repository.
  • General Documentation: For further details on project guidelines, data integration, and model development, visit our complete Documentation section.

Getting Started

  1. Read the project overview and familiarize yourself with our mission.
  2. Join our community channels on EBRAINS and GitHub Discussions.
  3. Explore our GitHub repository for open issues and tasks.
  4. Begin contributing and share your expertise!
