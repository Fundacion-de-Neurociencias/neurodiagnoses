Get Involved with Neurodiagnoses

Join our collaborative effort to advance AI-powered diagnostics for complex CNS diseases.

Ways to Contribute

  • Participate in Discussions: Connect with us on our EBRAINS Forum to collaborate and share insights. Join our GitHub Discussions for in-depth technical conversations.
  • Contribute to Code Development: Visit our GitHub repository to report issues, suggest features, or submit pull requests. Your expertise in AI, neuroscience, or software development is invaluable.
  • Share Data and Resources: Collaborate by providing datasets, tools, or other resources that can enhance our diagnostic models.
  • Run Your Own AI Training: Utilize our open-source framework to train AI models on your data. Follow these steps:
    1. Clone the repository from GitHub.
    2. Install the required dependencies.
    3. Access our Jupyter Notebooks for model training and analysis.
  • Stay Updated: Follow us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates and opportunities to get involved.
