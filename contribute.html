Get Involved in Neurodiagnoses
# Contributing to Neurodiagnoses Neurodiagnoses is an **open-source AI diagnostic framework** promoted and supported by **Fundación de Neurociencias** ([FNeurociencias.org](https://www.fneurociencias.org)). This project brings together **clinicians, researchers, and AI developers** to build a next-generation diagnostic system for complex CNS diseases. We encourage contributions from: ✔ **Neurologists & Radiologists** – Clinical validation & case studies ✔ **AI Researchers & Data Scientists** – Machine learning, deep learning, feature engineering ✔ **Medical Informatics Specialists** – Integrating AI models with clinical workflows ✔ **Neuroscience & Bioinformatics Experts** – Biomarker discovery & neuroimaging 📌 **Fundación de Neurociencias supports this initiative** to advance neuroscience research and AI-driven diagnosis. 🔗 **Learn more about Fundación de Neurociencias:** [FNeurociencias.org](https://www.fneurociencias.org)

How to Contribute

Neurodiagnoses is an open collaboration for AI-powered CNS diagnostics. We welcome **researchers, AI developers, neuroscientists, and clinicians**.

💡 Why Join?

By participating in Neurodiagnoses, you contribute to **next-generation AI models for diagnosing CNS diseases**.

📌 Ways to Get Involved

  • 💻 **AI Development:** Train and optimize machine learning models.
  • 🧠 **Neuroscience Research:** Contribute to the integration of biomarkers and neuroimaging.
  • 📊 **Data Science:** Work with EEG/MRI/biomarker datasets.
  • 📜 **Scientific Writing:** Help document findings and publish research.
