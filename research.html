Our Research

At Neurodiagnoses, we are dedicated to integrating artificial intelligence with neuroscience to revolutionize diagnostics and treatment strategies for central nervous system (CNS) disorders.

Key Research Areas

  • AI-Based Annotation System – Automating structured diagnostic reporting through advanced machine learning techniques.
  • Probabilistic Diagnosis Modeling – Utilizing AI to predict disease progression and assist in patient stratification.
  • Neuroimaging and EEG Data Analysis – Extracting critical biomarkers from MRI and EEG data to enhance AI training models.
  • Federated Learning & Secure Data Sharing – Enabling multi-center AI training while ensuring data privacy and security.
  • Integration of Neuromarker Ontology – Standardizing biomarker classification across neurodegenerative diseases to improve diagnostic accuracy.
