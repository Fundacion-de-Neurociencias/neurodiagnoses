Neurodiagnoses Research

Our Research

Our work focuses on the integration of artificial intelligence with neuroscience to enhance diagnostics.

Key Research Areas

  • 📌 **AI-Based Annotation System** – Automates structured diagnosis reporting using machine learning.
  • 📌 **Probabilistic Diagnosis Modeling** – Predicts disease progression and patient stratification.
  • 📌 **Neuroimaging and EEG Data Analysis** – Extracts biomarkers from MRI and EEG for AI training.
  • 📌 **Federated Learning & Secure Data Sharing** – Enables multi-center AI training without data exposure.
