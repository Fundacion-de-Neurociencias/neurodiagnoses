Reimagining Neurodegenerative Diagnostics

Diseases of the Central Nervous System (CNS) like Alzheimer's or Parkinson's are incredibly complex. Neurodiagnoses aims to unify heterogeneous data—from MRI scans and CSF tests to cognitive assessments and genetic profiles—into a coherent, probabilistic framework.

High-Level Architecture

The project's vision is a unified system that processes diverse inputs through a sophisticated pipeline to generate flexible, clinically relevant outputs.
