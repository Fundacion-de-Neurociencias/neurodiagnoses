Reimagining Neurodegenerative Diagnostics

Diseases of the Central Nervous System (CNS) like Alzheimer's or Parkinson's are incredibly complex. Diagnoses often rely on scattered, heterogeneous data—from MRI scans and CSF tests to cognitive assessments and genetic profiles. Neurodiagnoses aims to unify this information into a coherent, probabilistic framework.

Key Functionalities

The project focuses on five core functionalities to provide a comprehensive diagnostic and prognostic platform:

  • Probabilistic Annotation – AI-based diagnostic modeling using probabilistic networks.
  • Tridimensional Annotation – Integration of neuroimaging, genetic, and clinical data.
  • Multi-Model Advanced Annotation – Unified pipeline for comprehensive 3-axis annotation.
  • Biomarker Prediction – AI-driven estimation of biomarker status and progression.
  • Disease Prediction – Modeling of disease onset, conversion, and progression using ML.
