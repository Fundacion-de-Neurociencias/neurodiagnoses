Reimagining Neurodegenerative Diagnostics

Diseases of the Central Nervous System (CNS) like Alzheimer's or Parkinson's are incredibly complex. Neurodiagnoses aims to unify heterogeneous data—from MRI scans and CSF tests to cognitive assessments and genetic profiles—into a coherent, probabilistic, tridimensional framework.

Key Functionalities

The project focuses on core functionalities to provide a comprehensive diagnostic and prognostic platform:

  • Probabilistic Annotation – AI-based diagnostic modeling using probabilistic networks.
  • Tridimensional Annotation – Integration of neuroimaging, genetic, and clinical data.
  • Biomarker Prediction – AI-driven estimation of biomarker status and progression.
  • Disease Prediction – Modeling of disease onset, conversion, and progression using ML.

High-Level Architecture

The project's vision is a unified system that processes diverse inputs through a sophisticated pipeline to generate flexible, clinically relevant outputs.
