About Neurodiagnoses

What is Neurodiagnoses?

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered diagnostic framework that integrates multi-modal data, including genetics, biomarkers, and neuroimaging, to improve the accuracy of CNS disease diagnosis.

This project was started on the 1st February 2025, therefore it is in its initial steps.

Why is This Important?

Traditional CNS disease classification relies on phenotypic observations, leading to misdiagnoses and overlapping syndromes. Neurodiagnoses shifts the paradigm to a **data-driven approach**, providing more reliable, precise, and scalable diagnostic solutions.
