About Neurodiagnoses

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered open-source framework designed to advance the diagnosis and prediction of central nervous system (CNS) disorders. The platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning techniques and integrates multi-modal data—including genetic, neuroimaging, neurophysiological, and biomarker datasets—to generate structured, explainable diagnostic outputs.

Three-Dimensional Diagnostic Model

  • Etiology: Identifying genetic, environmental, and molecular contributors to disease.
  • Pathophysiology: Analyzing molecular markers, pathways, and disease mechanisms.
  • Neuroanatomoclinical Presentation: Utilizing neuroimaging, clinical symptoms, and physiological markers.

Technologies and Platforms

Neurodiagnoses integrates with:

  • EBRAINS: High-performance computing and neuroimaging data repository.
  • Clínica.run: Advanced neuroimaging processing.
  • The Virtual Brain: Simulations for modeling brain dynamics.

Research Focus Areas

  • CNS Digital Twins: Combining omics with neuroimaging and digital health records.
  • AI-Driven Risk Stratification: Predicting disease progression and therapeutic response.
  • Real-Time Clinical Decision Support: AI-based diagnostic aids for EHR systems.

Future Directions

Neurodiagnoses aims to extend into real-time clinical decision support systems, personalized medicine approaches, and automated analysis for continuous discovery in neuroscience research.
