About Neurodiagnoses

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered open-source diagnostic framework designed to enhance the accuracy of diagnosing complex central nervous system (CNS) disorders. The platform integrates multi-modal data—combining clinical biomarkers, neuroimaging, and genetic information—with advanced machine learning models.

Neurodiagnoses offers a dual diagnostic approach:

  • Probabilistic Diagnosis: Generates multiple potential diagnoses with associated probability percentages to aid in differential diagnosis.
  • Tridimensional Diagnosis: Structures the diagnostic output based on three axes: etiology, molecular markers, and neuroanatomoclinical correlations.

In addition to its clinical application, Neurodiagnoses is expanding into a research platform to develop CNS Digital Twins. This research dimension will integrate omics data (proteomics, genomics, lipidomics, transcriptomics), various neuroimaging modalities, and digital health records. The goal is to model disease progression, identify novel biomarkers, and explore new therapeutic targets.

Neurodiagnoses is promoted and supported by Fundación de Neurociencias, a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing neuroscience research and AI-driven diagnostics.
