About Neurodiagnoses

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered open-source framework integrating **probabilistic modeling, machine learning, multimodal data (neuroimaging, biomarkers, and clinical data), and language models** to enhance **diagnostic precision and disease tracking**.

Data Integration and Models

  • EBRAINS: High-performance neuroimaging and clinical data retrieval.
  • Clínica.run: Advanced neuroimaging processing.
  • Hugging Face: NLP-based diagnostic annotation and AI models.
  • The Virtual Brain: Brain dynamics simulations.
  • InterMine: Multimodal data warehouse.
  • BIDS Standardization: Structuring neuroimaging data for interoperability.

Three-Dimensional Diagnostic Model

  • Etiology: Genetic, molecular, and environmental contributors.
  • Pathophysiology: Biomarker-based disease tracking.
  • Neuroanatomoclinical Presentation: Neuroimaging, symptoms, and physiological markers.

Research and Diagnosis

  • CNS Digital Twins: Integrating omics, neuroimaging, and digital health records.
  • AI-Driven Risk Stratification: Predicting disease onset and therapy response.
  • 3D Diagnostic Annotation: Merging PET, MRI, EEG into anatomical overlays.
  • Machine Learning for Disease Progression: Training models for real-time tracking.
