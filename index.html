About Neurodiagnoses

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered open-source framework designed to advance the diagnosis and prediction of central nervous system (CNS) disorders. The platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning techniques and integrates multi-modal data to generate structured, explainable diagnostic outputs.

Three-Dimensional Diagnostic Model

  • Etiology: Identifying genetic, environmental, and molecular contributors to disease.
  • Pathophysiology: Analyzing molecular markers, pathways, and disease mechanisms.
  • Neuroanatomoclinical Presentation: Utilizing neuroimaging, clinical symptoms, and physiological markers.

What is Neurodiagnoses?

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered framework that integrates probabilistic modeling, machine learning, and multimodal data (neuroimaging, biomarkers, and clinical data) to enhance diagnostic precision and disease progression tracking.

The project focuses on five core functionalities:

  • Probabilistic Annotation - AI-based diagnostic modeling.
  • Tridimensional Annotation - Integration of neuroimaging, genetic, and clinical data.
  • Interactive Visualization - User-friendly interfaces for research.
  • Biomarker Prediction - AI-driven estimation of biomarker status.
  • Disease Prediction - Modeling of disease onset and progression.
