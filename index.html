Welcome to Neurodiagnoses

An AI-powered tridimensional diagnostic framework for complex CNS diseases.

What is Neurodiagnoses?

Neurodiagnoses is an AI-powered framework that integrates probabilistic modeling, machine learning, and multimodal data (neuroimaging, biomarkers, and clinical data) to enhance diagnostic precision and disease progression tracking.

The project focuses on five core functionalities:

  • Probabilistic Annotation – AI-based diagnostic modeling using probabilistic networks.
  • Tridimensional Annotation – Integration of neuroimaging, genetic, and clinical data.
  • Interactive Visualization – User-friendly interfaces for research and clinical applications.
  • Biomarker Prediction – AI-driven estimation of biomarker status and progression.
  • Disease Prediction – Modeling of disease onset, conversion, and progression using ML.

How You Can Contribute

We are currently in the early phases of development, focusing on documentation, planning, and initial model implementation. Based on your background and interests, you can contribute in various areas:

  • Documentation and Organization – Review and improve documentation on GitHub and eBrains.
  • Development and Modeling – Implement probabilistic models, develop AI-based prediction algorithms, and integrate MRI/PET models with clinical data.
  • Platform Development – Build APIs and web services, and develop an interactive visualization interface.
  • Analysis and Validation – Benchmark diagnostic models with open datasets and perform validation with ADNI, UK Biobank, and other repositories.
